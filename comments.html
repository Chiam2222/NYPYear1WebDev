I Love Traveling

Post by Owner, Oct 30, 2019.
Travel Love

Traveling is my passion. My dream is to travel around the world and explore the beauty of the world. I have been to many country such as China, Taiwan, Malaysia, Japan, Australia, Indonesia, Thailand. My plan for my next year is to go Korea. Planned in the right way, travelling can be about so much more than just crossing places off your bucket list. There is lots to learn when visiting other countries. Open yourself up to new experiences – stay in a local home stay, volunteer with a community charity, get to know the people and the culture of each new place you visit – and you’ll find that you return home, not only with lots of great memories but a lot of new skills too. Travel Experiences give us the ability to experience the local culture while traveling in a relaxed way. No line-ups, no waiting, always easy and always fun!

Officially Blogging

Post by Hunyeong, May 15, 2020.
Japan

The best time to visit Japan is from March to May during the late spring. You can also plan a visit during late autumn, especially in the months from September to November for the best experiences. The temperature remains mild during this time with very little rainfall.

Are you looking for beautiful places in Japan? Here are the best places to visit in Japan that you should include on your itinerary to make the best of your trip. These are what all awaits you in the scenic land!

  • Tokyo – Essence Of Japan
  • Kyoto – Sacred And Serene
  • Mt. Fuji – A Breathtaking Marvel
  • Hokkaido – Closer To Nature
  • Hiroshima – Historically Significant City
  • Sapporo – Forget The Heat And Humidity
  • Osaka – A Cultural Delight
  • Takayama – Away From City Life
  • Asakusa – For Parties And More
  • Nagasaki – A City With Sad History
  • Hakone – Views Of Mount Fuji
